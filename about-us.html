About Us

Ocean Dolphin

We encourage students to pursue their aspirations and aid them in doing so. We have the greatest country and course, according to the students’ past studies. Based on our knowledge, we give students with the appropriate approach to their classmates. With the help of a dedicated, educated, and inspired team of more than 25 workforces, we provide services for countries such as the United Kingdom, United States of America, Canada, Australia, Ireland, New Zealand, Poland, Dubai, and Europe visas in a timely, straightforward, more open, secure, and seamless manner.

Ocean Dolphin is the best top consultancy in Surat that provides student visas. We strive to provide the best service possible to our students. Education is the passport to, the future for tomorrow, let’s prepare today to fly tomorrow.

We provide high-quality services to students and professionals interested in studying abroad. Our services include assisting students in making the best decision possible based on their qualifications, work experience, course preferences, budget, and other requirements, as well as providing personalised counselling on courses and countries, as well as visa procurement and post-landing services.

Vision

To be the leading Overseas Education Consulting Organization, to create success stories for our students, value additions to our partner institutions.

Mission

Commitment to growing our business with an emphasis on Excellence, Honesty, Integrity & Updated Knowledge. To build strong and credible relationships with partner institutions. To provide customized solutions to students aspiring to study overseas.

Objective

Ocean Dolphin aims to achieve the mission and vision through the following objectives :

  • To provide key staff members with the necessary expertise and skills, as well as to professionally improve them so that they can deliver high-quality services to students.
  • To meet or exceed the requirements set out by globally recognized regulatory or certifying bodies to be eligible to provide recruiting services.
  • To get hold of official representation status from Institutions of a global reputation and introduce them to Indian students, As a result, students will be able to participate in their chosen classes while still helping universities achieve their cultural integration goals.
  • To actively participate in the marketing plans of principal foreign Institutions by conducting events and providing infrastructure and administrative facilities as well as supplying market intelligence.
  • To have language proficiency training for Indian students wishing to seek higher education abroad.
  • To assist and allow students to achieve desired language outcomes, aptitude, and competitive entrance tests.
  • Counseling and advising applicants on the most fitting classes and institutions for their academic credentials, as well as assisting them with the admissions process.
  • To provide solutions that are systematic for searching programs, identifying Institutions, improving language proficiency, facilitating applications, scholarships & funding, visa assistance, pre-departure preparations, accommodations, and post-landing under the same roof.

Maintaining relationships with family members during recruitment, as well as collecting input from students to help prospective students.

Ocean Dolphin represents various high-ranked universities across different countries: Australia, Canada, New Zealand, UK, Europe, USA, and Singapore.
