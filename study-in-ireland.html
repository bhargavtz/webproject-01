Why Canada As Study Destination

Ireland’s education system is hailed across the world for both its quality and quantity. The country offers a broad array of courses, ranging from sciences to commerce to arts, making it an ideal choice to study in Ireland for Indian students. Ireland’s higher education system is classified into 4 major categories for specialized training and academic growth of the students.

(A) Universities

There are a total of 9 universities to study in Ireland for Indian students offering degrees at Bachelor’s, Master’s and Doctorate levels.

(B) Institutes of Technology

Ireland hosts a total of 14 Institutes of Technology that offer a variety of programmes at degree, national diploma and national certificate levels.

(C) Colleges of Education

Ireland has set up 5 Colleges of Education for primary school teachers. These colleges offer 3-year full time programmes leading a B.Ed. degree.

(D) Independent Third Level Colleges

These are colleges that offer certificate, diploma, and degree programmes recognized by the country’s national certification authority.

Work Opportunity In Ireland

Ireland has a diverse industry but still, there are some industries which have a greater role in Ireland economy Like IT & Pharma. These are the two primary industries which give a contribution of more than 50% to Ireland’s economy which clearly state that exposure in this field is abundant. This vast industry provides large job opportunities to the students.

The world’s top IT & Pharma companies like Google, Microsoft, Facebook, Intel, Johnson & Johnson, Roche, Pfizer etc. are established in Ireland. Courses & programmes in Irish universities go parallel along with the IT & Pharma industries, which help to develop the required skill set in the students and prepare them for direct college placement and other job opportunities in these companies. Irish universities provide the 100% English courses with great options so the language is not the constraint now. Simple knowledge of English is sufficient to study there.
